NPWP, or Nomor Pokok Wajib Pajak, is a 15-digit Tax Identification Number in Indonesia. It serves as a unique identifier for taxpayers, facilitating the management of tax rights and obligations. Purpose of NPWP

The NPWP is essential for both individuals and businesses operating within Indonesia's tax system.
It ensures compliance with tax obligations and allows access to tax-related services.


Who Needs an NPWP?

- Individuals: Workers with fixed incomes, business owners, freelancers, married women separating assets, and heirs of undivided estates.
- Corporations: All companies and government institutions must have an NPWP to conduct business legally.
